**About the Project:**
Respiratory disease is the 3rd leading cause of death worldwide, yet diagnosis often relies on the subjective human ear. This tool uses **Gemini 3 Proâ€™s native multimodal audio capabilities** to:
1.  **Visualize** raw clinical audio with real-time spectrograms.
2.  **Analyze** pathology (wheezes/crackles) with millisecond precision, correcting messy human labels.
3.  **Isolate** the signal by generating custom Python filters to remove noise instantly.
